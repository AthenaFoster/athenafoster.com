Athena Foster lives in the woods of Wisconsin dodging faerie rings, writing speculative fiction, and performing bureaucromancy during the weekday. She also works as a creative producer, creating folio editions and merchandise for authors. She studied creative writing at the Universities of Iowa and Wisconsin and regularly plays table-top and live action role-playing games. At last report, she is defending a 19th century farmhouse against a colonizing horde of pernicious woodchucks.
