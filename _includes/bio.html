Athena Foster is an American author of speculative fiction. Born in Des Moines, Iowa, she went away to college at the University of Wisconsin–Madison and never escaped Madison (though she did escape the UW with a degree in English). Along the way she worked in 2-4 gaming stores, played an egregious number of table-top RPGs and LARPs, and spent five years on the convention committee for ICON (Iowa's Longest Running Science Fiction Convention).
